What is An Around the Way Girl

Around the Way Girl is a rap song created by professionally known as LL Cool J in 1990. The song is an ode to stlye, grace, and unique beauty of what LL Cool J coins as an Around the Way Girl. A girl that is from his neigborhood and at that time period was not depicted in mainsteam media as the epitope of beauty. The cover art of the song reflects who LL Cool J views are encapsulate the essence of an Around the Way Girl. The song is meant to uplift and inspire these woman to continue to be themselves and reaffrims their identity.
